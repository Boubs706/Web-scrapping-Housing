<h2>Buildings with Mandates</h2>
<p>Buildings with mandates have special requirements for people who live in
their buildings, called “mandates.” For example, some mandates require that
tenants be seniors, artists or member of a specific cultural community.</p>
<p>Below are two lists for buildings with mandates. The first list includes
buildings with senior mandates and explains the process to be approved for a
mandate. The second list includes buildings with all other mandates and explains
the process.</p>

<h2>All Other Mandates</h2>
<p>The following are the steps to apply for a building’s mandate:</p>
<ol>
<li>Log in to your <a
href="https://www.rentcafesocialhousing.com/onlineleasing/city-of-toronto-rent-
geared-to-income-application/guestlogin.aspx" style="background-color:
\#ffffff;">MyAccesstoHousingTO</a> account to add the building(s) with the
mandate that you are interested in to your list of “Building Selections”.  This
can be done through the Building Complexes tab.</li>
<li>Contact the Housing Provider of the building you have selected. They will
then verify the eligibility for the mandate. You will need to provide
information required for the approval.</li>
<li>If approved, you will be able to view the mandated building(s) and express
interest when vacancies become available.</li>
</ol>
